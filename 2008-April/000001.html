Hi,

The purpose of this email is to kickstart the discussion on this list.
To begin, I suggest we agree on basic rules concerning modifications to
the SVN repository.  Here are my suggestions:

a) Patches to trunk should be discussed on this list before being commited;

b) Patches to trunk should be independent.  Therefore, "I have a patch that
   does A, B, and C" is not encouraged; it is better to post them independently
   (assuming A, B, and C are independent, of course);

c) The 'branches' tree is open to experiments and/or demonstrating proposed
   patches to trunk;

d) For the time being, the pgocaml-lwt patches by Jérôme Vouillon can reside
   in branches.  However, due to their usefulness to Ocsigen users, I suggest
   we make them available as a tarball alongside trunk's.


Any other points that should be addressed?

Cheers,
